About US

Us Villains are a 3-piece math-punk band with electro injected into it.
We are from Ontario (Canada, not California).

j72 plays guitar and sings, Edwin plays drums and sings, and Dorian plays bass.

j72 uses a CNZ Audio jazzmaster, a Tracer stratocaster, and a Solo custom stratocaster.
Edwin uses a Roland RD 150, an Edifier R1280T, and some drums.
Dorian uses a P-Bass. Any will do.
